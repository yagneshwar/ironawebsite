Frequently asked questions with solutions

References

What is an art board ?

Art board is a kind of switchboard which has a front end( wall interface) and the back end (Hub).

How does the art board look on my wall ?

Art board look can be decided by the user. We provide few categories of solutions for your art board.Categories: Include Normal wall paint, Wall art, wallpaper, wall sticker

What are the operating options for art board ?

There are three operating options for art board.

Through Mobile app.

Through manual switches

Through touch interface

What to do when the touch interface is not responding ?

on the hub you see a push button which will reset the device,click on it. If still not responding operate with mobile app (or) manual switches.If either of options not working raise issue with us through mobile app/call

What to do when the appliances connected to the art board start flickering?

check the cable connection with the hub is well placed or not. If not On the hub you see a push button which will reset the device,click on it. If it is still flickering switch off the device from automatic mode to manual mode which is on your hub and use your board through manual switches.Raise a request through your app.

What is sensitivity?

Sensitivity is an operating value for the touch interface. When a person touches the interface,it detects a value to make the appliance. That value is known as the sensitivity value.User can adjust sensitivity value through mobile app.

What if the art board is not responding with all the three ways of switching?

check whether there is a power supply in the house and see whether led is “on” on device.

If the led is off, the issue is with the power supply to the hub (or) house.

If led is on and you are not able to operate switches in any way click on the button on hub to reset the device.

If still the issue exists,raise an issue to our support team through app/call.

What are manual switches ? How to operate them ?

Manual switches are normal switches attached to our hub. It is a three way switch with 3 modes of operation (Automatic Mode, Manual mode and off). You can change the mode of operation on your interest.You can see on which mode the switch is with the signs on the hub.

Can i control/monitor in the cloud ?

Cloud is not implemented for this model. We would update this product with cloud for next versions.

Can i control the art board with Google mini/ Alexa/ siri ?

Cloud is not implemented for this model.We would update this product with cloud for next versions. (Including mini,alexa,siri and other Hub’s)

What is the maximum Load capacity of your device ?

For each switch 2Amps is the Load capacity, 12 Amps is the load capacity of our device.

I wanted to change my wall art/wallpaper/wall sticker/normal wall paint. What can i do ?

To change your wall art/wallpaper/wall sticker/normal wall paint you can raise through app for interface change through mobile app/call. If you are using other vendor/ doing yourself, for interface work make sure wall art/wallpaper/wall sticker/normal wall paint is cleared well and see sensors will not be disturbed during work.

My sensors are not working after the change of wall interface ?

(i) If warranty exists and wall interface change is done by us. We will provide a free service and solve the problem.

(ii) If warranty exists and wall interface work done by other vendor/person/by user. Service charges will be applicable.

(iii) If warranty doesn’t exists and wall interface work is done by us. Service charges will be applicable according to the problem user face.

(iv) If warranty doesn’t exists and wall interface work done by other vendor/person/by user. Service charges will be applicable.

What is the product warranty for art board ?

1 year product and service warranty for the product with applicable terms and conditions.

Mobile App Questions

How to connect Iconnect app and art board through my mobile?

Turn on bluetooth in your device and check for bluetooth name “HC-05” in available devices. Click on bluetooth name “HC-05” and provide password (1234 (or) 0000) then click on pair/connect.

What if the bluetooth device HC-05 is not found in available devices ?

just refresh near the available devices and switch off and on it again. If still not found reach out to our customer support team

What to do when the password (1234 (or) 0000) say's wrong?

Switch off and on bluetooth again and try it. If still you are not able to connect to board reach out to our customer support team

What to do when an app crashes while opening (or) while operating iconnect app?

close your app and Clear cache in your mobile and try opening/using the app again. If it is still not working, uninstall the app, clear the cache and reinstall the app from playstore. If problem exists even after installation reach out to our customer support team

How to add room in the iconnect app?

After opening the app you see blank space (or) Hub for a new user(If you already added any room you can see the added room name in the space) with the "+" symbol at the bottom right corner. click on the "+" sign and you see two fields ( enter room name & enter static IP). Enter room name of your interest and static IP provided by our technician, click on submit You can see room added in hub

What if the added room name is not found in the hub?

If an added room is not found in the hub try adding once again, If still not found close the app, clear the cache and try adding room. uninstall and reinstall the app. if not solved contact our customer support

I am trying to operate the art board through the iconnect app but it is not working?

(i) Check your bluetooth whether it is connected to your art board device After clicking on the room you see a toast message connected to room(acc. to name you specified).

(ii)If it is still not working close app,clear cache and open the app and try to operate appliances.

(iii)uninstall and reinstall the app and check, If still issue persists raise it through app/ call our support team.

How to change name and icon for icons (light/fan/any other) in an iconnect app?

Long press on the icon you want to change, You see two fields (icon name and select your icon). Change icon name and click on "down arrow" symbol to change the icon symbol,click on ok

To confirm changes click on the "reload" arrow at the right corner of the app interface.

What is sensitivity value and where can I change it?

Sensitivity value is the touch operating value of an art board which you can change by yourself. click on the room which you want to change sensitivity value, At the bottom of page you see sensitivity option just click on the toggle and enter sensitivity value, click on submit

How can i change the room name and MAC address?

Long press on the room name and you see “ edit” symbol click on it and you see 2 fields (room name and mac address), Just add both and save it

How to delete a room ?

Long press on the room and you see delete symbol.click on delete you see a message asking for confirmation just click yes the room will be deleted.
